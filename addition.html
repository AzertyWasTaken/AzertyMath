Addition

Definition

Addition is an operation that combine two numbers to make a third one.

This operation is denoted with the + symbol.

The result of addition is called the sum.

Properties

  • Addition is commutative:

    • \(a + b = b + a\)

  • The identity of addition is 0:

    • \(n + 0 = n\)
