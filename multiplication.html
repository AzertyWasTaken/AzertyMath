Multiplication

Definition

Multiplication is an operation that indicates how many times a number is added to itself.

This operation is denoted with the Ã— symbol.

The result of multiplication is called the product.

Properties

Multiplication is commutative: changing the order of its numbers does not change the result.

Any number multiplied by 0 is equal to 0.

The product of any number by 1 never change its result.

The product of two negative numbers is a positive number.
