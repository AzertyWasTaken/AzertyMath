Parity

Definition

The parity of an integer is the property of being odd or even.

A number is even if it is divisible by \(2\).

A number is odd if it is not divisible by \(2\).

Properties

Addition

The sum of two even numbers is even.

The sum of an even number and a odd number is odd.

The sum of two odd numbers is even.

Subtraction

The difference of two even numbers is even.

The difference of an even number and a odd number is odd.

The difference of two odd numbers is even.

Multiplication

The product of two even numbers is even.

The product of an even number and a odd number is odd.

The product of two odd numbers is odd.
