Prime factorization existence

Statement

Every positive integers have a prime factozation.

Proof

2 is prime so it is already a product of prime numbers (just itself).

Let \(n\) be a positive integer \(\gt\) 2.

Assume that every integer \(\gt\) 2 and \(\lt n\) can be written as a product of prime numbers.

  • If \(n\) is prime, we are done.
  • If \(n\) is not prime, it is the product of two numbers \(a\) and \(b\), that are both \(\gt\) 2 and \(\lt n\).

    • Since these numbers can be written as a product of prime numbers, \(n\) is also a product of prime numbers.
