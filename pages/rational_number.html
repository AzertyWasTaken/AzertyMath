Rational number

Definition

Rational numbers are numbers that can be expressed as the fraction of .

Rational number can also be negative.

They are written on the form \(\frac{p}{q}\).

Properties

\(\frac{a \times n}{b \times n} = \frac{a}{b}\)

Operations

Addition

\(\frac{a}{b} + \frac{c}{d} = \frac{a \times d}{b \times d} + \frac{c \times b}{d \times b} = \frac{a \times d + c \times b}{d \times b}\)

Subtraction

\(\frac{a}{b} - \frac{c}{d} = \frac{a \times d}{b \times d} - \frac{c \times b}{d \times b} = \frac{a \times d - c \times b}{d \times b}\)

Multiplication

\(\frac{a}{b} \times \frac{c}{d} = \frac{a \times c}{b \times d}\)

Division

\(\frac{a}{b} \div \frac{c}{d} = \frac{a}{b} \times \frac{d}{c} = \frac{a \times d}{b \times c}\)
