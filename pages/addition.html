Addition

Definition

Addition is the way we find out what number we have in total when we put numbers together.

This is one of the four basic operations.

Addition is denoted with the \(+\) symbol.

The result of addition is called the sum.

Addition can also be thought as repeated successions.

\(3 + 2 = S(S(3))\).

Where \(S(n)\) is the successor operation.

Properties

  • Addition is commutative:

    • \(a + b = b + a\)

  • The identity of addition is 0:

    • \(n + 0 = n\)
