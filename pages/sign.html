Sign

Definition

The sign of an integer is the property of being negative or positive.

A number is positive if it is greater than \(0\).

A number is negative if it is less then \(0\).

Properties

Multiplication

The product of two positive numbers is positive.

The product of a positive number and a negative number is negative.

The product of two negative numbers is positive.

Division

The fraction of two positive numbers is positive.

The fraction of a negative number and a positive number is negative.

The fraction of two negative numbers is positive.
